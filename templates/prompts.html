- Meta App: Central dashboard linking all scrapers, tracking leads, generating emails
- City Scrapers: Focus on hurricane-prone cities (Houston, Charleston, Wilmington, etc.)
- Industry Scrapers: Target high-value sectors (hotels, casinos, hospitals, schools)
- Business Type Scrapers: Find decision makers (architects, contractors, developers)

TARGET LEAD PROFILE:
- Companies with large glass surfaces
- Located within 50 miles of coast
- Recent permits for construction/renovation
- Insurance claims for storm damage
- Projects over $500k value

EMAIL STRATEGY:
Each lead gets personalized outreach mentioning:
- Their specific project/location
- New code requirements
- Miami-Dade certification importance
- Cost of non-compliance

TECHNICAL APPROACH:
- SerpAPI for broad searches
- Playwright for permit databases
- Public records for recent construction
- LinkedIn for decision makers
- Google Maps for coastal proximity

SUCCESS METRICS:
- 2,000 qualified leads by end of weekend
- 10,000 leads by end of October
- Emails with 5%+ open rate
- Focus on quality over quantity

Remember: We're not just finding glass buyers - we're finding companies that WILL NEED certified hurricane glass due to codes, insurance, or storms. We reach them first with education + solution.
